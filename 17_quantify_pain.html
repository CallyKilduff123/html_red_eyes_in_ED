Question: How severe is the pain?



Moderate to severe pain

This may be accompanied by sensitivity to light (photophobia). Features include waking the patient from sleep, interfering with ADLs and poor response to analgesia.

Mild pain / discomfort

No red flag features for pain. Eased by analgesia, not interfering with ADLS. A good pain history will help differentiate the type and extent of the pain.
