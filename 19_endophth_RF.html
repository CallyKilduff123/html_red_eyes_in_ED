Question: Are there risk factors for endophthalmitis?



Risk factors present

Risk factors include: Recent eye surgery or injection (but can occur late), trauma to the eye, corneal ulcer, previous glaucoma surgery with filtering bleb (these are often superior to cornea, and are best seen by lifting upper lid).

No obvious risk factors

No previous ocular surgery, injection or trauma. No corneal ulcers. Systemically well and immunocompetent.
