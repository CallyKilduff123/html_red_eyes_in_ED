Question: Is there proptosis present?



Proptosis present

Also known as exophthalmos. The eye appears to be protruding forward from its natural position. Please note: in thyroid eye disease, there may be bilateral proptosed red eyes

No proptosis

There does not appear to be any obvious proptosis or bulging of the eyes.
