Question: Do the eyelashes appear to be touching the eyeball?



Eyelashes are touching the eyeball

The eyelashes may be growing towards the eye (trichiasis), or the eyelid may be turned in (entropion). The cornea may stain with fluorescein.

Eyelashes are not touching the eyeball

There does not appear to be any obvious inturning of the lid or misdirection of eyelashes.
