Question: Is the discharge hyperpurulent?



Hyperpurulent discharge

Thick pus that may reform on wiping. Conjunctival chemosis and eyelid swelling may be present. These findings may be unilateral.

Purulent discharge

Discharge from the eyes is yellow/green pus, eyes may be stuck together in the morning, with eyelash crusting. The pus does not reform soon after wiping.
