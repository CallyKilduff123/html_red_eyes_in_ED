ALLERGIC

CONJUNCTIVITIS


May be seasonal. Itching (sore eye afterward), watery discahrge, chemosis (swelling of conjunctiva), swollen eyelids, papillae on lid eversion, mild diffuse redness ("pink eye") +/- history of atopy, excema, asthma, hayfever.

PF Lubricants QDS. g. Olopatadine BD (GP to continue 4/12). Allergen advice - avoid triggers and avoid eye rubbing. PO antihistamines e.g. Fexofenadine (counsel re: sedative effect).

If persistent or severe (corneal staining on fluorescein) - routine referral to ophthalmology

Ensure no red flag features.
